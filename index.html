Otome Games, Wikipedia article

Otome games are story based romance games who's market audience are women. These games follow a female mc with mostly male characters in which the player can try to develop a romantic relationship with. They're usually presented as visual novels or dating sims in which the choices you make can affect the story and outcome. The games and genre originated in Japan and the word "otome" translates to maiden game.
